Education

Master's Degree in Mathematics (2018, TU-Berlin)

Master's Thesis: Fast Approximation of Equations of transient Gasflow

The thesis compares different algorithms to approximate time-dependent gas flow in natural gas networks. I present different linearizations of the nonlinear gas flow equations and evalute the model performances.

Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics (2016, TU-Berlin)

Bachelors's Thesis: Algorithmic testing of equivalence of polynomials to the determinant

The thesis gives a detailed description and analysis of a randomized algorithm from "Affine Projections of polynomials" (Kayal, 2012) which can be used to solve the equivalence problem for the determinant polynomial in polynomial time.
